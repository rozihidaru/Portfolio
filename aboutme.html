Rozi Hidaru: About Me

Hello! My name is Rozi Hidaru. I'm a rising senior at Lakeside School in Seattle, Washington. I play basketball and I run cross-country and track. I am also the president of Lakeside's Black Student Union, and I am a member of the Student Health Advocacy Committee and the Student Affinity Leadership Team. In my free time I like to read, spend time with my three younger siblings, and watch TV.
